# ⚡ AggressiveAlpha — Deployment Guide
## Access from anywhere — phone, tablet, desktop

---

## WHAT YOU NEED (all free)
1. GitHub account — github.com
2. Railway account — railway.app (sign up with GitHub)

---

## STEP 1 — Upload to GitHub (5 minutes)

1. Go to github.com and sign in
2. Click the "+" button (top right) → "New repository"
3. Name it: aggressivealpha
4. Set to Public
5. Click "Create repository"
6. On the next page, click "uploading an existing file"
7. Upload ALL these files keeping the folder structure:
   - package.json        → root level
   - server.js           → root level
   - public/index.html   → inside a folder called "public"
8. Click "Commit changes"

---

## STEP 2 — Deploy on Railway (5 minutes)

1. Go to railway.app
2. Click "Login" → "Login with GitHub"
3. Click "New Project"
4. Click "Deploy from GitHub repo"
5. Select your "aggressivealpha" repository
6. Railway automatically detects it's a Node.js app
7. Click "Deploy Now"
8. Wait about 60 seconds for deployment

---

## STEP 3 — Get your URL

1. After deployment, click on your project
2. Click "Settings" → "Networking" → "Generate Domain"
3. You'll get a URL like: https://aggressivealpha-production.up.railway.app
4. Open that URL in any browser — your dashboard is live!

---

## STEP 4 — Use it on your phone

1. Open your URL in Safari (iPhone) or Chrome (Android)
2. Tap the Share button → "Add to Home Screen"
3. Now you have an app icon on your phone!
4. Tap it anytime to open your dashboard

---

## HOW TO USE THE DASHBOARD

1. Open your Railway URL
2. Tap "SCAN 100 US STOCKS"
3. Wait about 2-3 minutes (fetching real Yahoo Finance data)
4. See your Top 10 buy signals with:
   - Live price
   - Entry / Target / Stop prices
   - 52-week range position
   - Dividend yield
   - RSI, Volume, Momentum
   - Confidence score
5. Tap "Log Buy" on signals you act on
6. Switch to "All Results" tab to see all 100 stocks ranked

---

## BEST TIME TO SCAN (Australian time)

NYSE/NASDAQ market hours = 1:30 AM – 8:00 AM AEST
- Scan BEFORE 1:30 AM AEST = plan for that night's session
- Scan AFTER 8:00 AM AEST = review yesterday's close, plan for next session
- Most useful: scan around 8-9 AM AEST with end-of-day prices

## TROUBLESHOOTING

App won't load?
→ Check Railway dashboard — make sure deployment is "Active" (green)

Scan takes too long?
→ Normal — fetching 100 stocks takes 2-3 minutes. Don't close the browser.

Some stocks show as skipped?
→ Normal — Yahoo Finance occasionally blocks certain tickers temporarily.
  Re-scan to try again.
